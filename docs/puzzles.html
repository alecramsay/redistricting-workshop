Redistricting Puzzles

The map shows 25 blocks arranged in a 5×5 grid, representing a simplified state with 15 cyan voters and 10 green voters. Your task is to divide the map into 5 districts of exactly 5 contiguous blocks each.

  • A district is won by whichever party holds a majority — at least 3 of its 5 blocks.
  • Districts must be contiguous: every block in a district must share an edge with at least one other block in that district.
  • The colored dot inside each block shows the block’s party. The background color shows which district it belongs to.

Select a district button, then click blocks to assign them. Clicking a block already in the active district removes it.
