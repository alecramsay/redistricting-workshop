Redistricting Puzzles

The map shows 25 blocks arranged in a 5×5 hexagonal grid, representing a simplified state with 15 orange voters and 10 green voters. Your task is to divide the map into 5 districts of exactly 5 contiguous blocks each.

  • A district is won by whichever party holds a majority — at least 3 of its 5 blocks.
  • Districts must be contiguous: every block in a district must share an edge with at least one other block in the same district.
  • The dot inside each block shows the block’s party (orange or green). The background color shows which district it belongs to.

Click a district row in the panel to select it, then click blocks on the map to assign them. Clicking a block already in the active district removes it.
